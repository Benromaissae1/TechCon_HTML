TechCon: From Humble Beginnings to Global Innovation Hub
TechCon started in the early 2000s as a small conference bringing together tech enthusiasts and startups. Its goal was simple: share new ideas and innovations.
As technology grew rapidly, TechCon expanded in size and scope through the 2000s and 2010s, adding keynote speakers, workshops, and exhibitions focused on emerging tech like AI and cloud computing.
Today, TechCon is a major global event held in multiple countries. It covers cutting-edge topics such as cybersecurity, quantum computing, and VR, attracting thousands of participants and streaming sessions worldwide.
